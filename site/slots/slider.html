A slider is a usefull user interface component displaying a list of items or only a part of, when the list is too long to fit on the main dimension in one of the two screen dimensions: vertical or horizontal the page and topics sliders used here are horizontal .

Selecting any of the displayed slides brings it in the front, i.e. focused in the center of the screen the associated zoom effect makes the selected front slide appear in front and outlined . The front slide always triggers a subsequent action when it is acted on for instance by a mouse click or a finger tap .

Sliders have, at their foot, a spot indicator to visually locate the slide position in the full list. Acting on these indicators spots the respective slide on the front. Slides can also be directly scrolled forward or backward for instance left or right .
